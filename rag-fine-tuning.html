RAG & Fine?Tuning: The AI Advantage for Your Business — Offline & Private

Two proven ways to get reliable AI results from your own data: Retrieval?Augmented Generation (RAG) and fine?tuning. If you’re not using them yet, you’re leaving accuracy, efficiency, and competitive advantage on the table.

What is RAG?

Retrieval?Augmented Generation connects an AI model to your private knowledge — policies, PDFs, wikis, tickets, contracts — so answers are grounded in facts. The model retrieves relevant passages first, then generates a response citing that context.

Why it matters

  • Reduces hallucinations with verifiable sources.
  • Uses your live documents without retraining the model.
  • Great for internal assistants, search, and customer support.

Offline & Secure

We index and query your data on your own servers. Content never leaves your environment.

What is Fine?Tuning?

Fine?tuning adapts a base model to your domain language and tasks using example data. The result: a model that understands your terminology, tone, and formats — performing better on specialised work.

Why it matters

  • Improves accuracy on domain?specific tasks.
  • Encodes preferred structure and style for outputs.
  • Pairs well with RAG for even better results.

Offline & Secure

We fine?tune on dedicated offline hardware. Training data stays private and compliant.

RAG vs Fine?Tuning — When to Use Which?

  • Start with RAG when answers must reference current documents, policies, or records.
  • Add fine?tuning when you need consistent formatting, domain?specific reasoning, or improved task performance.
  • Use both for the best of both worlds: live factual grounding + domain?adapted behaviour.

What You’re Missing Without Them

  • Hours lost searching documents and rewriting content by hand.
  • Inconsistent outputs and avoidable mistakes.
  • Knowledge trapped across silos that AI could surface instantly.
  • Opportunities to re?use IP in proposals, reports, and support.

Offline & Secure by Design

  • On?prem or edge deployment — suitable for air?gapped networks.
  • No data leaves your infrastructure by default.
  • Access control, audit trails, and evaluation dashboards.

How We Deliver

  1. Discovery — map use?cases and success criteria.
  2. Data readiness — connect sources; clean and secure.
  3. Pilot — RAG baseline; optional fine?tuning dataset build.
  4. Evaluation — measure accuracy, latency, and UX.
  5. On?prem deployment — containerised serving, access control, monitoring.
  6. Enablement — handover, documentation, and training.
