Intelligent Meeting Services

Transform your meeting recordings into actionable intelligence. By combining our high-accuracy voice-to-text transcription with RAG, we can analyze discussions, cross-reference them with your internal documents, and provide comprehensive insights.

Key Features:

  • Automated transcription of meeting audio and video.
  • AI-powered summaries highlighting key decisions and outcomes.
  • Automatic detection and listing of action items and deadlines.
  • Answers questions about the meeting by referencing the transcript and related documents.

RAG for Business

Retrieval-Augmented Generation allows your AI to access and reference your internal company data, providing accurate, context-aware answers from your private knowledge base.

Key Benefits:

  • Leverages existing documentation and data.
  • Reduces model "hallucinations" with factual grounding.
  • Ensures data privacy by using in-house information.
  • Powers intelligent, internal-facing chatbots and search tools.
