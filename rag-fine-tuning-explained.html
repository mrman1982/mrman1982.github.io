RAG: Making AI Actually Useful for Your Business

What is RAG?

RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Think of it as giving AI access to your company's "brain"-all your documents, policies, procedures, historical data, and institutional knowledge. Instead of generic responses, RAG-powered AI can:

  • Answer questions using your actual company data
  • Reference specific policies and procedures
  • Provide context-aware insights based on your industry
  • Cite sources from your internal documents

The Business Impact

Real Business Scenarios Where RAG Transforms Operations:

  • Customer Support: Instantly find relevant policy information and past case resolutions
  • Compliance: Get accurate, source-cited answers about regulations and procedures
  • Training: New employees can ask questions and get answers from your knowledge base
  • Decision Making: Access historical data and insights to inform strategic choices
  • Research: Quickly synthesise information across multiple internal documents

Without RAG vs. With RAG

? Generic AI (Without RAG)

"Based on general knowledge, most companies handle refunds by..."

? RAG-Powered AI

Fine-Tuning: Teaching AI to Speak Your Language

What is Fine-Tuning?

Fine-tuning takes a pre-trained AI model and teaches it the specific language, terminology, and patterns of your industry and business. It's like hiring a specialist instead of a generalist-the AI becomes an expert in your domain.

The Transformation

Industries That Benefit Most from Fine-Tuning:

  • Healthcare: Medical terminology, treatment protocols, diagnostic procedures
  • Legal: Legal jargon, case law references, contract language
  • Finance: Financial regulations, risk assessments, compliance requirements
  • Manufacturing: Technical specifications, safety protocols, quality standards
  • Real Estate: Property descriptions, market analysis, regulatory compliance

Generic AI vs. Fine-Tuned AI

? Generic AI Response

"A construction delay might be caused by weather, materials, or labor issues..."

? Fine-Tuned Construction AI

"Based on the site conditions and current material delivery schedules, this appears to be a Type 3 delay under clause 15.2 of the standard contract. Recommend implementing the accelerated schedule protocol and notifying the client within 48 hours per company policy..."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RAG and why do businesses need it?

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) allows AI to access and reference your company's internal documents and data, providing accurate, context-aware answers grounded in your actual business information rather than generic responses.

What is fine-tuning and how does it help my business?

Fine-tuning customises AI models to understand your industry's specific terminology, processes, and requirements, dramatically improving accuracy and relevance for your particular use cases.

Can these AI improvements be deployed offline?

Yes. We specialise in offline, on-premises deployments so your sensitive business data never leaves your secure environment while still getting cutting-edge AI capabilities.

How long does implementation typically take?

Most RAG and fine-tuning projects take 2-8 weeks depending on complexity and data volume. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

What kind of data do I need for RAG and fine-tuning?

For RAG: documents, policies, procedures, FAQs, and knowledge bases. For fine-tuning: examples of your industry-specific language, terminology, and communication patterns.
